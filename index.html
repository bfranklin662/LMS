Privacy Policy
Last Man Standing

How we use your data

Polytechnic FC uses your details to run the Last Man Standing competition (creating your account, managing entries, contacting you about your picks and competition admin).

What we collect

  • Name, email, phone, team/club connection
  • Your picks and outcomes

Why we collect it

  • To run the competition and contact you about your entry
  • To administer results and verify eligibility

Legal basis

Consent (and legitimate interests for competition administration).

Who we share it with

Only the competition organisers and the services used to host the site/API.

Retention

We keep data for the duration of the competition and a reasonable period after for admin/audit.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion by contacting the organisers.
